Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan was a Jedi Master and general during the Clone Wars era of Star Wars. He was the master of Anakin Skywalker, who would later fall into the Dark Side and transform into iconic villain, Darth Vader. After the fall of the Republic and Jedi massacre at the end of the Clone Wars, Obi-Wan retreated to a desert planet named Tatooine. He stayed here to watch over one of Anakin's children, Luke Skywalker. He'd eventually join Luke to battle the Empire and sacrifice himself on the Death Star.
